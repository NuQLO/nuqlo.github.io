Welcome to NuQLO at PUC-Rio, a research group focused on quantum communication and computation. We are dedicated to advancing quantum technologies and exploring their practical applications. Our team of scientists and engineers conducts innovative research, collaborating with institutions to drive progress in the field. Through our work, we aim to shape the future of quantum technology and inspire the next generation of researchers and engineers.

At NuQLO, our research spans various areas, including quantum communication protocols and computation algorithms. We strive to develop secure communication channels and harness the immense computational power of quantum computers. By fostering collaborations and sharing knowledge, we aim to accelerate the development and deployment of practical quantum technologies. Join us on this exciting journey as we unlock the potential of quantum communication and computation at NuQLO, based at PUC-Rio.
